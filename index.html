What is Red River Festival?

The Red River Festival is a vibrant, family friendly celebration that brings people from both sides of the Texas/Oklahoma border together for a weekend of music, flavor, and good old fashioned fun! Whether you are showing off your tossinng skills in the cornhole tournament or taste testing your way through smoky barbecue masterpieces, theres something for everyone to enjoy.

Here's a taste of whats happening:

  • Food trucks galore: From street tacos to deep fried delicousness
  • Live music: See your favorite artists right in your community.
  • Beer garden: A relaxing spot to sip and socialize
  • BBQ contest & cornhole tournament: compete or cheer on the contenders!
  • And much much more!
  • All ages welcome: With activities designed for families and festival goers of all kinds

Its more than just a festival - Its a way to celebrate community, culture, and the spirit of the Texas/Oklahoma rivalry. Ready to join the fun? We can't wait to see you there!
